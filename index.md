## About Me

I have an undergraduate degree in **Computer Science** from **Ryerson University** in Toronto, and it was there that my passion for **User Experience** and **User Centered Design** was born.

I started to notice my frustrations with using different systems around me, and thought there had to be a better way. Some googling led me to discover UX design, and I immediately fell head over heels with it.

During my undergrad, I worked on an undergraduate thesis in **Human Computer Interaction**. I had the chance to conduct **user research**, **interviews**, and **testing**, as well as create and iterate over wireframes and designs using **Sketch**, **Framer**, and **Principle**.

Coming from a development background, I understand the constraints of a system and can help design not just for users, but for the people developing the software as well.
